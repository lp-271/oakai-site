Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Oak AI ("we," "us," or "our") provides AI-powered customer communication services, including SMS text messaging, voice calls, and appointment scheduling, on behalf of local businesses that we partner with (each a "Client Business"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with an AI assistant operated by Oak AI on behalf of a Client Business.

Information We Collect

When you contact a Client Business through our AI assistant via phone call or text message, we may collect:

  • Your phone number
  • The content of your messages or call transcripts
  • Appointment details you provide, such as preferred date, time, and service requested
  • Any other information you voluntarily share during the conversation

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your questions about the Client Business
  • Schedule, confirm, or modify appointments
  • Send appointment reminders and confirmations
  • Send a follow-up message after your appointment to request feedback
  • Improve the quality and accuracy of our AI assistant

Information Sharing

We share your information only with the specific Client Business you contacted, so they can fulfill your request (such as booking your appointment). We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information with other Client Businesses.

Text Messaging

By providing your phone number and messaging a Client Business, you consent to receive text messages from that business via our platform, including appointment confirmations, reminders, and occasional follow-up messages. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data Retention

We retain conversation data only as long as necessary to provide our services to the Client Business, or as required by law.

Your Rights

You may request to have your information deleted or ask what information we have on file by contacting us at the email below.
