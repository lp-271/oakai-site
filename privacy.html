Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

📵 No mobile phone numbers or SMS opt-in data collected through this service will ever be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

Oak AI, a service of Oak Services LLC ("Oak AI," "we," "us," or "our"), provides AI-powered SMS text messaging and voice call communication services on behalf of local businesses ("Client Businesses"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you interact with our AI assistant on behalf of a Client Business such as ShortCuts Barber Shop.

1. Description of SMS Service

Oak AI operates an AI-powered text messaging assistant for local businesses. When you text a phone number connected to our service, you will receive automated responses from an AI assistant that can answer questions, provide business information, and schedule appointments. Message types include:

  • Responses to customer inquiries about hours, services, and pricing
  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Post-visit follow-up messages requesting feedback
  • Google review requests for satisfied customers
  • General business information and directions

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with the service, typically 2-5 messages per interaction or appointment cycle. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

2. Information We Collect

When you contact a Client Business through our AI assistant via SMS or phone call, we may collect:

  • Your mobile phone number
  • The content of your text messages
  • Appointment details you provide (preferred date, time, service requested, barber preference)
  • Your feedback responses (thumbs up/down replies)
  • Call transcripts when you interact with our voice assistant
  • Any other information you voluntarily share during the conversation

We collect this information when you initiate contact by texting a Client Business phone number or scanning a QR code that opens a text message to that number.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Respond to your questions about the Client Business
  • Schedule, confirm, modify, or remind you of appointments
  • Send a brief follow-up message after your visit to request feedback
  • Send a Google review link to customers who indicate a positive experience
  • Improve the accuracy and quality of our AI assistant
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your conversation data is processed through secure, encrypted channels. We use industry-standard technical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Conversation data is retained only as long as necessary to provide our services, typically no more than 12 months, unless a longer period is required by law.

5. Information Sharing — Mobile Data

No mobile phone numbers, SMS opt-in data, or message content collected through this service will be shared with third parties, affiliates, or lead generators for marketing or promotional purposes at any time.

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With the Client Business you contacted — so they can fulfill your appointment or service request
  • With service providers — such as SMS delivery platforms (Twilio) and AI processing (Anthropic), strictly to operate the service on our behalf. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and may not use your data for their own purposes
  • If required by law — in response to a valid legal request, court order, or to protect safety

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your information with other Client Businesses. We do not share your data with advertisers.

6. SMS Opt-In and Opt-Out

By texting a Client Business phone number powered by Oak AI, or by scanning an Oak AI QR code that initiates a text message, you consent to receive SMS responses and follow-up messages from that Client Business via our platform.

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive one confirmation message and no further messages will be sent. To opt back in, reply START.

For help, reply HELP to any message or contact the Client Business directly.

7. Your Rights and Data Requests

You have the right to:

  • Request to know what information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@oakai.net.

8. Children's Privacy

Our SMS services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information through our service, contact us at contact@oakai.net and we will promptly delete it.
